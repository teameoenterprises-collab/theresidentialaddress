Physical Phone FAQ

Can a physical US phone be used alongside my local country SIM?

+

Yes. You can use our physical US phone as a secondary device or swap the SIM into a dual-SIM compatible phone. You can keep your physical local SIM card active for data and personal calls while having our US SIM active simultaneously to receive 2FA codes and business communications.

Is my phone compatible?

+

Our physical phone is a basic, unlocked device that will work anywhere. If you wish to use your own device, it must be carrier-unlocked (not tied to a specific local provider contract) and support T-Mobile's network bands.

How does "WiFi Calling" work for roaming?

+

To avoid massive international roaming fees, keep data roaming off for the US eSIM. When connected to regular WiFi or hotspots in your home country, the phone routes SMS and calls seamlessly over the internet to the US provider, pretending you are physically standing in America.
