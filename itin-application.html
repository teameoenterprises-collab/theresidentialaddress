ITIN FAQ

Do I absolutely need an ITIN to open a US Bank Account?

+

No. You can open commercial accounts at Mercury or Relay with just your LLC's EIN and your foreign passport. However, an ITIN is required to open personal US bank accounts, access US credit cards (like Capital One or Amex), and create advanced Stripe/PayPal accounts without limitations.

Can I use my National ID card instead of a Passport?

+

The IRS requires two forms of identification if you do not use a Passport. A Passport is the only standalone document that proves both your identity and your foreign status. We highly recommend using a valid, unexpired passport.

Does an ITIN make me liable for US personal taxes?

+

No. Possessing an ITIN does not automatically trigger US tax residency or make you liable for US income tax. It is simply a processing number for the IRS to track informational filings and tax treaty claims. Your tax liability is determined by your physical presence (Substantial Presence Test) and your business's "Effectively Connected Income" (ECI).
